[1mNAME[0m
       relocate - relocate a company to a more profitable location

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
       [1mrelocate [-c|-s] [4m[22mfile[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
       [1mrelocate  [22mRuns  the  relocation  program using the `Company Description
       File'

[1mOPTIONS[0m
       -c --complex
              relocates the company  specified  by  the  `Company  Description
              File' taking into account the weight of deliveries

       -s --simple
              relocates  the  company  specified  by  the `Company Description
              File' without taking into account the weight of deliveries

       -h --help
              display this help and exit

       -v --version
              output version information and exit
